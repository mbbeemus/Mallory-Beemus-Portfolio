About Mallory Beemus

I am a dedicated student at James Madison University, pursuing a degree in Media Art and Design with a concentration in Interaction Design, complemented by a minor in Computer Science. As a proud member of the Honors College, I strive for excellence in all my academic and extracurricular endeavors. I am secretary of Boarderline, the official Ski and Snowboard Club of JMU and treasurer of the Media Art and Design Club.

On top of my academic work, I am heavily involved in organizations around campus. I am secretary of Boarderline, the official ski and snowboard club at JMU. As secretary, I am responsible for facilitating communication and managing over 350 members. In my role as secretary, I have developed the offcil website for the club and began leading a member-run newletter to further communication with alumni, parents, and other non-members. In addition to my role at Boarderline's Secretary, I am tresurer of the Media Art and Design Club. As treasurer, I maintain the funds of the club as well as organizaze and lead fundraisers for the club.

I specialize in web programming and design, with a strong focus on front-end development. My technical skills include proficiency in HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, which I leverage to create engaging, user-friendly web interfaces. Additionally, I have solid experience in back-end development using Java, Python, and C++, enabling me to build reliable and versatile applications.

Throughout my academic journey, I have successfully developed and launched several websites, showcasing my ability to translate ideas into functional and aesthetically pleasing digital experiences. My projects, available on GitHub, reflect my commitment to quality and my continuous pursuit of innovation. My projects include but are not limited to a mock-up website for a swimwear company and the official website for the James Madison Ski and Snowboard Club, Boarderline.

My passion extends to the realms of User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design. I am dedicated to understanding user behavior and crafting intuitive designs that enhance the overall user experience. My approach combines creativity with analytical thinking, ensuring that my designs are both beautiful and effective.
