Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator Projects

Where I am From

In this project, I was tasked with creating a single, cohesive image that represents where I’m from, using a combination of several personal images. The base of this composition is my bedroom, the place where I study, sleep, work, and live. On the bed, I blended an image of the polaroids I have on my wall into the bedding. These polaroids capture memories of friends, both past and present, reflecting the journey that has shaped who I am today.

In the window, I included an image of Mount Baker in Washington State. Although I’ve never lived in Washington, this mountain holds personal significance. When I visited a few years ago, I was struck by its immense beauty, and the memory of that experience has stayed with me ever since.

In this project, I used layer masks and transform tool to adjust the polaroids to flow with the bedding's edges and texture. For the window, I used adjustment layers such as contrast and brightness to make the image of Mount Baker integrate smoothly with the existing light in the room.

JMU Club Wrestling Logo

I was commissioned by the JMU Club Wrestling Team to design a custom logo. They wanted a design similar to that of the VA Wrestling logo, incorporating elements unique to their own team and JMU’s branding. For this project, I utilized advanced masking tools to carefully edit an action shot of JMU club wrestlers into the outline of a Virginia state map. This allowed me to create a seamless and dynamic visual representation that highlights the team’s energy and connection to their state and university. The final design captures the spirit of the club while maintaining a cohesive look.

Girls On The Run Marketing Project

For this project, I was challenged with creating a rack card, social media posts, and an alternate media design for the Girls on the Run coaching recruitment in the Shenandoah Valley Region. I started this project with research on Girls on the Run and their brand guides. I utilized similar design tactics as seen on their websites and social media. I utilized Adobe Photoshop to create the tote bag mockup and social media post mockups. Indesign was used to put together the social media posts and rack cards.

OThis projects challenged in in all degrees. I was working for a large nonprofit organization that needed to market towards adult women in the ages of 18-25. I put together my skills in Adobe, creative advertisement, and communications to create a fun, welcoming campaign to recruit Girls on the Run Coaches in the Shenandoah Valley.
